CoRide - Ride Sharing App

Team
3
Rating
8/10
Cost
$0

CoRide is a **ride-sharing app** designed to provide a low-cost alternative to taxis and similar services. The goal was to allow users to **find available rides** in their area, see open seats, and check the **cost per seat** before booking.

The app enables **drivers** to register their vehicle, set available **seats**, and determine a **price per ride**. Passengers can **search for rides**, select a **route**, and view **details about the car** (e.g., model, color, license plate) to identify it easily.

CoRide was developed using .NET MAUI, making it compatible with both **Android** and **iOS**. The project was built by a **team of 3** and took approximately **3 months** to complete.

The app was intended to be **easily accessible on the Play Store** and aimed to disrupt traditional taxi services by offering **more affordable pricing** and **peer-to-peer ride sharing**.

This project required work on **backend logic, UI design, and database management**, ensuring that the system **properly connected drivers with passengers**.

Future improvements could include **real-time ride tracking**, **a rating system for drivers and passengers**, and **better route optimization** for cost-effective rides.
