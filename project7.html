Project Overview

FWTHORN documents my personal journey toward healthier habits. Every day I record a short-form video that captures training highlights, honest nutrition tracking and a mood check-in. Viewers can mirror the prompts and submit their own results, which I aggregate inside a shared notion dashboard.

The concept blends entertainment with accountability: by openly publishing my calorie logs, weight trends and macro targets I lower the barrier for others to follow along. Weekly recap posts surface the best community clips and keep the momentum going.

Highlights

  • Daily prompts that guide followers through movement, hydration and reflection.
  • Shared Google Sheets tracker with auto-generated streak trophies.
  • Sunday recap episodes featuring community shoutouts and the upcoming focus theme.
