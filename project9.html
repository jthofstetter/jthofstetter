Project Overview

RealityCheck captures everything I eat, how I move and how I feel. The system turns those inputs into trendlines, highlights hidden patterns and issues a "keep going" or "adjust course" recommendation each week. Users can dig into macro projections or simply follow the next best step suggested by the app.

Long-term motivation comes from transparency: RealityCheck doesn't sugarcoat the outlook. If current habits point toward weight gain or energy dips, the graphs and copy say it plainly and provide specific counter-measures.

Highlights

  • Meal logging with barcode scanning and automatic macro classification.
  • Integrations for wearables and smart scales to keep data entry effortless.
  • Weekly "RealityCheck" report featuring trajectory charts and habit suggestions.
