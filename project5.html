This project focused on leveraging AI-driven automation to generate content and gain reach on social media platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. By using AI-powered tools, I was able to create and optimize short-form content that performed surprisingly well within a short period.

One of the key experiments was the "SneakLaker" YouTube channel. Within just 10 days, the account reached 50,000 views across multiple videos. The videos were automatically generated using Opus Clips, an AI tool that detects highlights in longer videos and extracts them into engaging short-form content with subtitles. This allowed me to quickly generate attention-grabbing clips that were ready for social media distribution.

The posting strategy involved uploading AI-generated clips with carefully selected hashtags to maximize discoverability. The results varied across platforms:

  • YouTube: The strongest platform with 50,000 total views over 10 days.
  • Instagram: Reached around 25,000 total views from Reels.
  • TikTok: Less engagement compared to Instagram and YouTube but still managed to gain 5,000 views.

This project showcased the potential of AI-powered content automation for social media growth. With minimal manual effort, AI was able to analyze and extract key moments from raw footage, apply subtitles, and prepare content for distribution. This proves that AI-based automation can significantly reduce content creation time while still generating meaningful reach.

The success of SneakLaker suggests that AI-assisted video editing and smart content strategies can be highly effective. In the future, improvements could include testing different AI models for clip selection, optimizing hashtag strategies further, and experimenting with different AI-generated voices for narration.
