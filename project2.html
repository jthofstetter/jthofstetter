Project Overview

AIvour turns AI art into a shoppable service. Visitors craft a prompt, choose parameters such as inference steps or delivery speed, and complete checkout using Shopify’s secure pipeline. Every order triggers an automated payload that lands in my processing queue, where I generate the imagery and deliver the results back to the client.

The entire journey was tuned for responsiveness, meaning the storefront stays as delightful on phones as it is on desktops. Clear copy, structured product pages, and subtle animations keep the brand cohesive while the backend handles payments and fulfillment seamlessly.

Experience Highlights

  • Custom product configurator that translates user prompts into structured orders.
  • Automated email handover so clients receive artwork with zero manual chasing.
  • Responsive layouts with micro-animations to reinforce a premium service feel.
