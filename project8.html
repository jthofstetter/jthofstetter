JustJoin Activities

A map-first social app where friends and new faces can launch spontaneous activities, coordinate logistics, and build micro-communities around shared interests.

Stage

Clickable prototype

Target Platforms

iOS · Android

Core Features

Activities · Chat · Map

Project Overview

JustJoin lowers the friction of getting people together. Hosts can drop a pin on the map, describe the vibe, limit seats and choose if the session is public or private. Participants tap to request a spot, see who else is attending and share live updates en route.

The experience balances safety and spontaneity: verified profiles, mutual friends and optional voice introductions keep things friendly while still making it easy to discover new circles.

Highlights

  • Map overlays showing trending hotspots and activities that fit your schedule.
  • Group chat with automatic context cards containing location, equipment and dress code.
  • Calendar sync plus reminders so nobody misses the meetup window.
